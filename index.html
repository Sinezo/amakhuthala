Taking these steps, a great design is born. A real world problem is stackled.

Planning & circuit analysis

Roughly draw the circuit from imagination and analyse the flow of current throughout the circuit.

Good analysis plays an important role as it gives the idea of a goal that is to be achieved and also helps in pinpointing troubles that could rise in implementation.

Simulation

Simulate the circuit, observe the components behaviour and take necessary measurements to have adequate data to ensure it meets the objective.

Simulation gives certainty and shows how all the components in the system communicate.

Implementation

Finally, connect all the components together in their respective positions and connect them in such a way that forms a loop(s) that can be easily closed and opened.

A good design should be user-friendly and be danger-free.
