About

Amakhuthala Electrical Design & Installation (AEDI) is an electrical business that focuses on the design as well as installation of electrical systems. In every design we employ the proven electrical theories of the greatest physicists and electrical engineers, like NIKOLA TESLA.

We value safety. In the process of designing and installation we follow NEC and IEC standards.

Our main objective is to outstand within the industry with the best quality and significance of our designs.
