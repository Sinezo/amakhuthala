E-LEARNING




Amakhuthala E-Learning is the initiave aiming to help students get better grades. We want students to get to understand the theory behind every concept, understand its laws and principles and be able to apply them effectively.

As stated above, our aim is not just to help students to pass with good grades. We want to help students digest what they are being taught in class and be able to apply that knowledge to solve real life problems.

HOW?


We all know that learning can be overwhelming sometimes. We as Amakhuthala, tried our utmost to make the learning fun, less overwhelming and fruitful at the same time.

  • We have cut our lessons into pieces that are easy to chew and swalow.
  • After each and every lesson there is a short test.
  • Students can ask for help anytime they encounter difficulties when studying.
